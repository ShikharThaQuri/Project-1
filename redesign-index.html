ABOUT OUR COMPANY

we are proud of our history. Our esteemed faculty, and our dedicated professionals, who, year after years, provide the environment and support to help our students realize their dreams.

New York School of Interior Design is a private, not-for-profit college devoted to the design of the interior enivironment. Our guiding principle is that the successful design of an interior space improves human welfare, and

That the interior design profession assumes an important role in ensuring that spaces are beautiful, functionla, healthy, safe, and built in a socially and environmentally conscientious way.

About Our Design

Chartered by the board of Regents of the University of the state of New York in 1924, NYSID is authorized by the Board of Regents to confer the Basic interior Design certificate (on-site and online), the degrees of Associate in Applied Science in interior Design, Master of Professional Studies in Lighting Design, and Master of Professional Studies in Sustainable Interior Environments.
